Spaghetti

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
spaghetti noodles 1 lb
pasta sauce large jar
parmesan cheese to taste
salt and pepper to taste

Step 1: Fill a large pot halfway with water. Add salt to water and allow to boil.

Step 2: Once water has reached a rolling boil, add pasta to water. Stir noodles to prevent sticking. Cook pasta for around 10 minutes.

Step 3: Pour pasta sauce into medium saucepan and heat on medium low until warm throughout.

Step 4: Once pasta is fully cooked, drain pasta. Serve with the warmed sauce and grated parmesan cheese. Add salt and pepper to taste.

PB&J Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
sliced bread 2 slices
strawberry jelly 1 tbsp
peanut butter 2 tbsp

Step 1: Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread until covered and speard evenly.

Step 2: Spread jelly on other slice of bread to get even coverage.

Step 3: Place two slices together and enjoy.

Note: If taking your sandwich to go, spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter on each slice of bread and proceed as normal. This should prevent the bread from getting soggy prior to mealtime.
